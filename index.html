My name is Christopher Williams

Hello and thanks for stopping by! My name is Christopher Williams and I was created in Orlando, FL 1990. As far as I can remember I have always had a passion for creation, art and technology. I've been writing songs since I was 9 years of age as well as producing music since I was around 14. Gaming has also always been a passion of mine, which ultimately pointed me towards my love for computers (as well as digitally producing instrumentals). About 5 years ago now, I began studying software development as well as game development and I instantly fell in love with the concept of creating something out of nothing just like music. Through studies on software development I also discovered web development, which is something I became just as drawn to as well as the rest of my studies. I started off with C++ and Visual Studio. I then went on to tackle Java, JavaScript, C#, HTML and CSS. I've always loved how well C# and the game engine Unity3D worked together. Through my studies of game development I also picked up the skill of pixel art as well as 3D artwork mainly using programs such as Aseprite and Blender. I've always felt that starting off with C++ really helped me out with understanding the languages I went on to study afterwards.

I moved from Orlando FL to Ogden UT in 2016 where I attended OWATC and enrolled in their software development program, where I was also formally introduced to the concept of web development (if you don't count making your MySpace page look super cool in middle school). Ever since Utah I have continued my studies on Game, Software and Web development. My main goal is to become a web developer for small businesses and my own gaming studio. I would also like to create an indie entertainment company that can showcase the work of all my creative friends and myself as well.
